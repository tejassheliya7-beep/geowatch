The Vision

Solving Urban Tracking Challenges

Tracking land use changes manually in rapidly growing cities like Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar is nearly impossible. Current methods are slow, labor-intensive, and prone to error.

GeoWatch uses state-of-the-art AI combined with High-Res Satellite Data from Sentinel-2 & Google Earth Engine to automate change detection in real-time.

Ensuring Smart Governance and Environmental Protection through automated urban planning compliance.
